Project Overview

Smartcare Systems is a specialized web-based solution designed for KENYA NATIONAL LIBRARY SERVICES HQ to streamline the lifecycle and maintenance of ICT equipment. From diagnostic logging to administrative oversight, this system ensured that a company’s digital infrastructure
stays operational through organized technician reporting and data-driven management
The primary goal was to replace manual logging with a digital "paper trail" for all ICT hardware. This allows the IT department to track the health of specific devices, monitor recurring hardware failures, and ensure only authorized personnel perform maintenance.
SmartCare was developed to solve the chaos of manual ICT maintenance tracking. It provided a centralized platform where technicians and administrators manage the entire lifecycle of hardware repairs and service requests.

Key Workflows

  • Role-Based Access: Separate dashboards for technicians and administrators.
  • Admin Approval: Admin authorizes new users and equipment additions.
  • Technician Search: Search for existing devices before creating new logs.
  • Lifecycle Management: Track devices from "Pending" to "Approved".
  • Secure Backend: Password hashing with relational MySQL database.

Project Overview

SmartAfya Clinic is a modern healthcare management system developed to help clinics and healthcare facilities digitize daily operations and improve patient care.

The platform centralizes patient records, appointments, pharmacy,emergency, reporting, and staff management into one secure system. It was created to reduce paperwork, simplify clinic workflows, and improve efficiency in healthcare environments.

SmartAfya combines healthcare and technology to provide a smarter, faster, and more reliable way of managing clinic operations. The system improves organization, enhances communication, and supports digital transformation in healthcare services. SmartAfya does not only manage patient care but also analyzes clinic performance through dashboards and audit logs, making it a comprehensive solution for modern healthcare management.

Core Features

    • Patient Management: Register and manage patient records securely.
    • Appointment Scheduling: Organize doctor and patient appointments efficiently.
    • Medical Records: Store and access patient medical history digitally and securely.
    • Staff Management: Manage clinic staff roles, responsibilities, and activities.
    • New Staff Registration: Allow administrators to register and manage new clinic personnel.
    • Pharmacy Management: Track medicines, prescriptions, and pharmacy operations.
    • Clinic Database: Centralized database for storing clinic information and healthcare data.
    • Audit & Monitoring: Track system activities and monitor user operations for accountability.
    • Dashboard Analytics: Monitor clinic performance through visual reports and insights.
    • Role-Based Access: Separate access levels for admins, doctors, and staff.
    • Secure Data Handling: Protect sensitive healthcare information and clinic records.
